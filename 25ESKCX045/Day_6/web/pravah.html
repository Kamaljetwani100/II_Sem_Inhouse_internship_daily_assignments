About Pravah

Tradition meets innovation

Pravah (प्रवाह) means "flow" — and that's exactly what the festival is: a current carrying music, sport, technology and craft through every corner of campus. Students, alumni and guests gather in a shared celebration, organised by the student council with every department lending a hand.

Organised by the College

From the opening parade to the closing concert, Pravah brings performances, workshops, competitions and new friendships to every attendee.
